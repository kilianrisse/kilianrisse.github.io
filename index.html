Kilian Risse

About

I am a graduate student in the Theoretical Computer Science Group at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. I am working on computational complexity, with a primary focus on proof complexity under the joint supervision of Per Austrin, Johan Håstad, and Jakob Nordström.
