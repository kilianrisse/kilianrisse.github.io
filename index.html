Kilian Risse

About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at LTH, Lund Univeristy hosted by Susanna de Rezende. Prior to this I was a postdoc at EPFL hosted by Ola Svensson and I have obtained my PhD from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden, under the joint supervision of Per Austrin, Johan Håstad, and Jakob Nordström.
