About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at LTH, Lund University hosted by Susanna de Rezende. Before that I spent some time at EPFL hosted by Ola Svensson. I completed my PhD at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden under the joint supervision of Per Austrin, Johan Håstad, and Jakob Nordström. I obtained my MSc and BSc from ETH Zürich.
